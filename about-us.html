About Us

Engage was founded by a team of seasoned professionals with a combined experience of over 3 decades in the finance industry. The firm was established with four core objectives:

  1. Corporate Services:

    To offer reliable bookkeeping solutions and provide training to finance teams, particularly for startups aiming to build strong financial foundations.

  2. Financial Advising:

    To offer accurate and impactful financial advise to individuals seeking.

  3. Graduate Upskilling:

    To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by delivering practical, industry-focused skill development programs to graduating students.

  4. Local Needs:

    To support Non-Resident Indians and Indians living in other states in managing their on-ground requirements in India or their origin state like banking and corporate services, assistance with official documentation, and support for their local family members.

  5. Career Counselling:

    To provide academic and professional counselling to navigate important decisions for career growth.
