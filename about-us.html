About Us

Engage was founded by a group of professionals who strongly believe in the need to bridge the corporate expectations of finance skill sets versus what the college graduates/semi-qualified professionals gain from the standard university curriculums,in addition to the fact that the corporates need support with the right skill set. "Engage" comes with providing support with a host of industry based services in various verticals.(Refer use cases under each services offering)

Our Core verticals-
  1. Corporate Accounting Services:

    Offer reliable bookkeeping solutions for start ups & established companies.

  2. Finance GCC Services:

    We provide end to end support from blue printing to establishing and stabilizing the GCC.

  3. Upskilling:

    To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by delivering practical, industry-focused skill development programs to graduating students/semi qualified/qualified professionals & working professionals.

  4. Support Local Needs:

    To support Non-Resident Indians and Indians living in other states in managing their on-ground requirements in India or their origin state like banking and corporate services, assistance with official documentation, and support for their local family members (Currently limited to Telangana, India).

  5. Career Counselling:

    To provide academic and professional counselling to navigate important decisions for career growth.
